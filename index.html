History of Cricket

Cricket ka janm 16th century England me hua tha. Shuruat me ye bachchon ka khel mana jata tha, lekin dheere-dheere ye puri duniya me popular ho gaya. 18th century tak Cricket England ka national sport ban chuka tha.

Important Milestones

  • 1844: Pehla International Cricket Match USA aur Canada ke beech hua.
  • 1877: Pehla official Test Match England aur Australia ke beech khela gaya.
  • 1971: Pehla One Day International (ODI) Australia aur England ke beech hua.
  • 1975: Pehla Cricket World Cup England me organise hua.
  • 2007: Pehla T20 World Cup South Africa me khela gaya.

Evolution of Formats

- Test Cricket: Sabse purana format, 5 din tak khela jata hai.
- ODI (One Day International): 50 overs ka format.
- T20 Cricket: Sabse fast format, sirf 20 overs.

Legends of the Game

Cricket history me kai mahaan players aaye, jaise Sir Don Bradman, Sachin Tendulkar, Brian Lara, Ricky Ponting, Jacques Kallis, Muttiah Muralitharan, MS Dhoni,Rohit Sharma,Virat Kohli.
